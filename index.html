Familia in STEM 💙

At SHPE-Berea, we draw inspiration from our culture to foster a supportive and inclusive environment for STEM majors at Berea College.

Since becoming an official chapter in 2023, we have been committed to empowering our members by organizing professional development events, attending conferences, and sharing professional development opportunities.

Our goal is to continue building a strong network that uplifts and supports Hispanic students in STEM fields.

Membership

SHPE National is a community of people like you who are looking to advance their professional lives alongside others. Whether you're interested in graduate school or industry, SHPE has something for you!

All on-campus events are free and open to everyone. By becoming an annual member, however, you will gain access to other benefits.

Student Membership Benefits:
  • Access to the SHPE National Convention
  • Networking opportunities with industry professionals
  • Scholarship and internship opportunities
  • Professional development workshops
  • Connection to a supportive community
  • Leadership development programs

  1. Join our chapter: Sign up through Berea College - Clubs and Organizations
  2. Become a SHPE National member: Visit SHPE.org to register
  3. Attend events: Participate in our meetings and professional development activities
  4. Get involved: Join committees and take on leadership roles

Undergraduate Membership: $10/year (National membership)

Local Chapter: Free for all Berea College students

National membership gives you access to exclusive resources, the annual convention, and career opportunities through the SHPE network.
