Familia in STEM



At SHPE-Berea, we draw inspiration from our culture to foster a supportive and inclusive environment for STEM majors at Berea College. Since becoming an official chapter in 2023, we have been committed to empowering our members by organizing professional development events, attending conferences, and sharing professional development opportunities.

Our goal is to continue building a strong network that uplifts and supports STEM Hispanic and non-hispanic students on their undergraduate journey at Berea.
